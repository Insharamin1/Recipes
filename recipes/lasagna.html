Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. In a large bowl, mix ground beef, onion, garlic, tomato products, spices and seasonings.
  3. Add ground beef to bowl and mix well.
  4. Add lasagna noodles and mix well.
  5. Add cheeses and egg.
  6. Pour lasagna noodles into bowl and cover with ground beef mixture.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes.
  8. Cool lasagna to room temperature.
  9. Slice lasagna noodles.
  10. Top with cheese and serve.
